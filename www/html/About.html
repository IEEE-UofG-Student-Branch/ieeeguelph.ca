Who we are
The IEEE is the largest technical professional organization dedicated to technological advancement. The University of Guelph IEEE Student Branch specifically is dedicated to helping engineering students advance their professional careers and achieve their personal goals.

We host a variety of events each year to help students achieve these goals, such as different workshops, guest speakers, job networking events, hackathons, coding competitions, and more. The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an international organization that aims to standardize and advance research in various electro-technologies such as quantity storage in computers, medical devices, and networking. Students who show interest and join the club will have the opportunity to attend various networking events, seminars, and workshops, providing students the opportunity to interact with engineers in various electricity related professions.
